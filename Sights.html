History of the Assyrian Empire:
Besides being well known for their superior army during its time, Iron weapons and building roads. Assyrians are said to be the first civilization. This structure does not look as tempting as the re-created one in the picture, however the amazing sight can be seen in modern Iraq. Ashur:
The city of Ashur is an ancient city that can be found in Qal'at Sherqat modern Iraq. The city comes to prove how civilizations were able to build advanced structures without the technology most of us think is needed. The city itself was named after the God Ashur, a god of war and the gods of gods.

Palmyra:
Palmyra or Nineveh Astrasouch is a structure used by the Assyrians back when the empire was at its peak. The structure is located in Syria close to Damascus. It was used as a theater and voting place for the Assyrians to make decisions.
